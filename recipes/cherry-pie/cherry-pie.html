Cherry Pie

Description

A delicous or disgusting cherry pie known from Mock Town. You have to try it for yourself to determine if its delicious or disgusting.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 60g weak flour
  • 60g strong flour
  • 80g salted butter
  • 10g milk
  • 1/2 egg
  • 15g granulated sugar
  • flour (as needed)

Custard Cream

  • egg yolks
  • 65g granulated sugar
  • 10g corn starch
  • 10g weak flour
  • 230mL milk
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 20g salted butter
  • 18 American cherries

Steps

  1. We’ll make the dough first, so mix the flour together and sift
  2. Cut butter into 1cm blocks
  3. Chill both flour mixture and butter in the fridge
  4. Mix milk, eggs, and sugar well, then chill in fridge
  5. Add the butter to the bowl with the flour chop-mix with a pastry cutter
  6. Pour in the egg mixture and mix together with a spatula
  7. Wrap in plastic wrap or parchment paper, and refrigerate 30 minutes to 1 hour
  8. Remove dough from refrigerato
  9. Place parchment paper on top of dough, and spread to proper size to fit in a pie pan with a rolling pin
  10. Place the dough in the pie dish, making sure that it is tightly against the bottom and pressed into the corners
  11. Use a floured fork and press along the walls of the pie dish, any dough sticking out over the edge should be cut off
  12. Using a fork, open 10 holes in the bottom
  13. Cover with parchment paper and fill with weight stones
  14. Preheat oven to 200°C. Cook 15 minutes
  15. Remove stones, reduce heat to 180°C and cook an additional 15 minutes, after cool on a cake rack
  16. In a pot, add egg yolk, sugar, cornstarch, weak flour, milk and mix
  17. Turn on stove, mix until thickened
  18. Remove from heat, add butter and lemon juice, and mix until smooth
  19. After cooled, pour into pie dish
  20. Remove cherry stems and cut cherries in half, remove seeds
  21. Place on top of the custard cream and chill in the refrigerator
